AfterNoon Safari

General description:

  • You will be picked up from your hotel and After a short drive, you will arrive at a station in the desert at 02:30 pm.
  • From here your adventure is starting, Take a thrilling ride in a jeep to a Bedouin village, Once arrived, a nice tea will be offered, You will have the opportunity to ride a camel and to visit a Bedouin pharmacy plus the Bedouin bread and how it is made.
  • Here you will get to know the culture and see how these people really life.
  • Time to go back to the station where you will have a thrilling ride on a quad for about 40 minutes. Drive through the sand dunes.
  • After going back to the station and taking a 15 min rest Then back to the hotel.
  • And of course, we will be waiting for your comment about the trip, and we look forward to seeing you on our other trips, and always we challenge the time to add something fun to your vacation.
